Game Modes

Find the Imposters

A fun party game for 3-20 players! Find the imposter who doesn't know the secret word. One player is randomly selected as the imposter and must blend in while others try to identify them through discussion.

Master of Words

A challenging word-guessing game! One player is selected as the "Master" and given a secret word. The Master must guide other players to guess the word through hints, while players try to figure it out within 5 minutes.

Silent Actor

A fun charades-style game! One player is selected as the "Silent Actor" and given a secret word or action. The Silent Actor must act it out without speaking for 1 minute while other players try to guess what they're acting out!
